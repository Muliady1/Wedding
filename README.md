# 💍 Wedding Invitation Website

A beautiful, elegant wedding invitation website built with Nuxt 3 and Vue.js. Create stunning digital invitations for your special day.

## ✨ Features

- **Beautiful Templates** - Elegant classic design with premium typography
- **Interactive Guest Book** - Guests can leave wishes and messages
- **RSVP System** - Manage attendance with ease
- **Photo Gallery** - Showcase your love story with a stunning gallery
- **Event Locations** - Google Maps integration for easy navigation
- **Gift Registry** - Bank account and digital wallet information
- **Music Player** - Background music for ambiance
- **Responsive Design** - Perfect on all devices (mobile, tablet, desktop)
- **Countdown Timer** - Live countdown to your special day
- **Multi-language Support** - Available in Indonesian and English
